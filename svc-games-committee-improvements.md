## **WHAT THEY'RE ACTUALLY TRYING TO ACHIEVE** üéØ

### The Big Picture Goals:
1. **"CELEBRATE/PLAY/EAT/STAY/BENEFIT LOCALLY"** - Keep visitor dollars in Silicon Valley instead of SF
2. **"Promoting One Silicon Valley"** - Unite the region as a single competitive destination  
3. **"Turn global moments into local wins"** - Convert massive international attention into local business opportunities

### Their 4 Core Activities:
1. **Event Coordination Hub** - Help businesses/communities organize watch parties, food fests, cultural celebrations
2. **Promotional Platform** - "The Guide to The Games" magazine to promote local businesses to visitors
3. **Networking/Partnership Building** - Monthly roundtables to connect businesses with each other and city officials
4. **Information/Resource Sharing** - Dos/Don'ts toolkit, updates from Santa Clara & Bay Area Host Committee

### What Success Looks Like For Them:
- Local businesses get more customers during the games
- Silicon Valley becomes known as THE place to experience the games (not just SF)
- Community events happen across the region (not just at the stadium)
- Businesses work together instead of competing
